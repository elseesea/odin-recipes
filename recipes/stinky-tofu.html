Stink Tofu

Description

The stinky tofu dish is a classic Taiwanese dish that nearly everyone loves.

It is fermented tofu with a strong odor. They usuall sold as snacks at night markets or roadside stands, or as a side dish to a meal.

The dish is made by fermenting in a brine with vegetables and meat, sometimes for months.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Brine
  • Vegetables
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Soak tofu in brine with vegetable and meat.
  2. Check weekly for fermentation progress.
  3. When the smell becomes unbearable and the look becomes extremely disgusting, it's ready to be fried.
