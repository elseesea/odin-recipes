Fried Egg

Description

The fried egg dish is a classic for humans around the world, except for vegans.

It is affordable, easy to cook to various doneness, and can be adorned with to one's fancy.

It provides good amount of protein, and can be a snack or a small meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a large skillet on medium high heat. Crack eggs directly into the pan. Cook to desired doneness.
  2. Lay cooked eggs on a plate. Season with salt and pepper. Eat.
